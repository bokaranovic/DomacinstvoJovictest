Uslovi naručivanjaOrdering Terms

Domaćinstvo Jović · posljednja izmjena: jul 2026.Domaćinstvo Jović · last updated: July 2026

Mi smo malo porodično domaćinstvo i najviše volimo kad se sve dogovori lijepo i po domaćinski — telefonom, uz povjerenje. Ovdje je ipak zapisano nekoliko jasnih pravila, da i vi i mi znamo na čemu smo.

1. Ko stoji iza narudžbe

Proizvode nudi Domaćinstvo Jović, porodična domaća radinost iz Drvara (Mlinski put 185, Drvar selo, Bosna i Hercegovina). Za sva pitanja tu je Suzana: +387 63 133 086 (telefon, Viber, WhatsApp) ili sue.jovic@gmail.com.

2. Kako nastaje narudžba

Narudžba poslata preko sajta, telefonom ili porukom je zahtjev za narudžbu, a ne konačan ugovor. Prije slanja svaku narudžbu potvrđujemo pozivom ili porukom — tada dogovaramo tačnu poštarinu, rok i način isporuke. Ako kupca ne uspijemo dobiti u razumnom roku, narudžbu ne šaljemo i ona se smatra otkazanom. Zato lažne ili šale radi poslate narudžbe ne mogu nikoga obavezati — ali nas usporavaju, pa vas molimo da naručujete ozbiljno.

3. Cijene i plaćanje

Cijene su iskazane u konvertibilnim markama (KM) i vrijede na dan narudžbe. Plaća se pouzećem — gotovinom kuriru pri preuzimanju — ili unaprijed po dogovoru. Poštarina nije uključena u cijenu proizvoda; saopštavamo je pri potvrdi narudžbe, prije nego što išta pošaljemo.

4. Isporuka

Šaljemo brzom poštom širom Bosne i Hercegovine, a u region po dogovoru. Pošiljku obično predajemo u roku od 1–3 radna dana od potvrde, a isporuka najčešće traje još 1–3 radna dana. Staklo pakujemo pažljivo, sa dodatnom zaštitom — kao da šaljemo rodbini.

5. Ako pošiljka stigne oštećena

Molimo vas da pošiljku pregledate pri preuzimanju. Ako je vidno oštećena, najbolje je da je ne preuzmete i da se šteta odmah zapisnički prijavi kuriru. Ako oštećenje primijetite tek kod kuće, javite nam se u roku od 48 sati sa fotografijama pošiljke i proizvoda — dogovorićemo zamjenu ili povraćaj novca. Šteta nastala u prevozu rješava se kroz reklamacioni postupak kurirske službe, a mi ćemo taj postupak voditi umjesto vas.

6. Povraćaj i reklamacije

Naši proizvodi su hrana i piće, pa otvorene proizvode iz higijenskih razloga ne možemo primiti nazad. Neotvoren proizvod u originalnom pakovanju možete vratiti u roku od 14 dana uz prethodni dogovor; troškove vraćanja tada snosi kupac. Ako smo mi pogriješili — poslali pogrešan artikal ili proizvod sa nedostatkom — sve troškove zamjene snosimo mi, bez pogovora.

7. Neopravdano odbijanje pošiljke

Ako je narudžba potvrđena, a kupac pošiljku bez razloga ne preuzme, zadržavamo pravo da tražimo nadoknadu troškova poštarine u oba smjera i da sljedeću narudžbu istog kupca pošaljemo tek uz plaćanje unaprijed.

8. Rakija i liker — samo za punoljetne (18+)

Alkoholna pića prodajemo isključivo punoljetnim osobama. Potvrdom narudžbe koja sadrži rakiju ili liker kupac potvrđuje da ima navršenih 18 godina.

9. Vaši podaci

Ime, telefon, adresu i e-mail koristimo isključivo za obradu i isporuku vaše narudžbe. Ne dijelimo ih ni sa kim osim kurirske službe (radi dostave) i ne šaljemo nikakve reklame. Na vaš zahtjev podatke brišemo.

10. Nesporazumi

Svaki nesporazum prvo rješavamo dogovorom — pozovite nas, dogovor je kod nas kućni običaj. Na sve što ovdje nije uređeno primjenjuju se propisi Bosne i Hercegovine, uključujući propise o zaštiti potrošača.

Slanjem narudžbe potvrđujete da ste ove uslove pročitali i da ih prihvatate. Uslove možemo povremeno dopuniti — važi verzija objavljena na ovoj stranici u trenutku naručivanja.

We are a small family household and we like things settled the friendly way — over the phone, with trust. Still, here are a few clear rules, so both you and we know where we stand.

1. Who is behind your order

The products are offered by Domaćinstvo Jović, a family homestead from Drvar (Mlinski put 185, Drvar selo, Bosnia and Herzegovina). For any question, Suzana is there for you: +387 63 133 086 (phone, Viber, WhatsApp) or sue.jovic@gmail.com.

2. How an order is made

An order sent through the site, by phone or by message is a request for an order, not a final contract. Before shipping, we confirm every order by call or message — that is when we agree on the exact shipping cost, time and method. If we cannot reach the customer within a reasonable time, the order is not shipped and is considered cancelled. Fake or prank orders therefore bind no one — but they do slow us down, so please order seriously.

3. Prices and payment

Prices are stated in convertible marks (KM/BAM) and are valid on the day of the order. Payment is cash on delivery — to the courier upon receipt — or in advance by arrangement. Shipping is not included in the product price; we state it when confirming the order, before anything is sent.

4. Delivery

We ship by express post across Bosnia and Herzegovina, and to the region by arrangement. We usually hand the parcel over within 1–3 working days of confirmation, and delivery typically takes another 1–3 working days. Glass is packed carefully, with extra protection — as if we were sending it to family.

5. If the parcel arrives damaged

Please inspect the parcel upon receipt. If it is visibly damaged, it is best not to accept it and to report the damage to the courier on the spot. If you only notice the damage at home, contact us within 48 hours with photos of the parcel and the product — we will arrange a replacement or a refund. Damage in transit is handled through the courier's claims procedure, and we will run that procedure on your behalf.

6. Returns and complaints

Our products are food and drink, so for hygienic reasons we cannot take opened products back. An unopened product in its original packaging can be returned within 14 days by prior arrangement; in that case the customer covers the return shipping. If the mistake is ours — a wrong item or a defective product — we cover all replacement costs, no questions asked.

7. Unjustified refusal of a parcel

If an order was confirmed and the customer fails to collect the parcel without reason, we reserve the right to claim the shipping costs both ways and to ship that customer's next order only against advance payment.

8. Brandy and liqueur — adults only (18+)

We sell alcoholic beverages exclusively to adults. By confirming an order containing brandy or liqueur, the customer confirms being at least 18 years old.

9. Your data

We use your name, phone, address and e-mail solely to process and deliver your order. We share them with no one except the courier service (for delivery) and we send no advertising. We delete your data on request.

10. Disagreements

We settle every disagreement by talking first — call us, that is the custom of our house. Anything not covered here is governed by the laws of Bosnia and Herzegovina, including consumer protection regulations.

By sending an order you confirm that you have read and accepted these terms. We may update them from time to time — the version published on this page at the moment of ordering applies.
